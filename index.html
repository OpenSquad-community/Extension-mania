About Extension Mania

Extension Mania is a one-of-a-kind event aimed at bringing together developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills in building innovative browser extensions. The event will take place at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM), and promises to be a thrilling and fun-filled experience for all participants.

What to Expect

  • Exciting challenges to create cutting-edge extensions that solve real-world problems
  • Access to state-of-the-art tools and resources, including expert mentors and workshops
  • Opportunity to network with like-minded individuals and showcase your skills
  • Grand finale to compete for exciting prizes

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in Extension Mania?

Extension Mania is open to all developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts who are interested in building browser extensions. However, participants must be at least 18 years of age and have a valid government-issued ID card.

What kind of extensions can I build?

The challenges at Extension Mania will cover a wide range of themes, such as text summarization, language translation, and more. You can choose to work on any of these themes or come up with your own innovative ideas.

How many members can be in a team?

Each team can have a maximum of 3 members.

Is there a registration fee for Extension Mania?

No, there is no registration fee for Extension Mania. Participation is completely free of charge.

What are the prizes for winning teams?

The top teams at Extension Mania will receive exciting cash prizes, along with certificates and recognition from our sponsors.
